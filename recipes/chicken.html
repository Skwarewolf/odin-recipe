Honey Glazed Chicken

Description

This recipe will excite and delight any table with the rich and sweet sauce over juicy, tender chicken cutlets. Easy, fast and versatile, this chicken dish is perfect for any family or just dining on your own.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1/8 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 1 1/2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves cut into bite-sized pieces

Directions

  1. Ready all your ingredients
  2. In a bowl, whisk together honey, soy sauce, and red pepper flakes; set aside for later
  3. Put your skillet on medium heat and add the olive oil. Toss your chicken in and brown lightly, about 5 minutes
  4. Add to the pan your honey mixture, continue cooking in sauce until chicken is fully cooked and no longer pink in the middle, roughly 5 more minutes
  5. Serve with your choice of rice, noodles or simply stand-alone and, most importantly, enjoy!
