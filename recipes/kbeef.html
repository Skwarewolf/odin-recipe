Easy Korean Ground Beef Bowl

Easy Korean Ground Beef Bowl

Description

This rich and hearty meal is sure to feel even the biggest of stomachs. Warm beef coated in a sweet and flavorful sauce atop warm, soft rice come together to fill bellies and hearts around your table.

Ingredients

  • 1lb lean ground beef
  • 5 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 tbsp freshly grated ginger
  • 2 tsp toasted sesame oil
  • 1/2 cup reduced-sodium soy sauce
  • 1/3 cup light brown sugar
  • 1/4 tsp crushed red pepper
  • 6 green onions, chopped and divided
  • 4 cups cooked brown rice
  • 1 tbsp toasted sesame seeds

Directions

  1. Gather and prep your ingredients
  2. Heat your skillet over medium-high heat. Add beef and cook, stirring and crumbling into small pieces until browned, then drain
  3. Stir in garlic, ginger, and sesame oil, cook a couple minutes until fragrant
  4. Add soy sauce, brown sugar and red pepper to pan, stir with beef until sauce is thick and absored; then add half your green onions
  5. Serve beef over hot, cooked rice; garnish with sesame seeds and the rest of the green onions
